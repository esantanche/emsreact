Class: InnerGridPane

InnerGridPane(classes, leftComponent, rightComponent)

This is the grid used for the main content. it shows two components side by side or one only on small devices. You can see it at work on the home page.

It shows two article cards or one on small devices.

leftComponent and rightComponent actually go one above the other on small devices.
